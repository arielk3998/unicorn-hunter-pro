## How It Works

### Step 1: Launch the Interview

Click the **"üéØ Career Interview"** button in the header of the Resume Toolkit.

### Step 2: Answer Questions

You'll be guided through 20 questions covering:

- **Career motivation** - What drives you in your work
- **Career stage** - Where you are in your journey
- **Work environment** - Startup, enterprise, agency, etc.
- **Team dynamics** - Team size and collaboration style
- **Leadership interests** - IC track vs management
- **Technical focus** - Specialization vs. generalist
- **Industry preferences** - Tech, healthcare, finance, etc.
- **Problem types** - What energizes you
- **Company stage** - Seed, Series A, public, etc.
- **Work location** - Remote, hybrid, office
- **Values alignment** - What matters most to you
- **Deal breakers** - What you won't accept

### Question Types

**Single Choice** - Select one option that best fits  
**Multiple Choice** - Select up to 3 options  
**Scale** - Choose where you fall on a spectrum  

### Navigation

- **Next ‚Üí** - Submit answer and move forward
- **‚Üê Back** - Return to previous question and change answer
- **Skip** - Skip optional questions

Your responses are saved automatically. You can:

## Tips for Best Results

### Be Honest

Answer based on what you truly want, not what you think you "should" want. Authentic responses lead to better matches.

### Think Long-Term

Consider where you want to be in 2-3 years, not just your next immediate role.

### Consider Trade-offs

No role is perfect. Think about what you're willing to compromise on vs. non-negotiables.

### Multiple Profiles

You might take the interview multiple times with different mindsets:
- "Dream job" profile - Ideal scenario
- "Pragmatic" profile - What's realistic right now
- "Pivot" profile - Exploring career changes

### Update Regularly

Your preferences change! Retake every 6-12 months or when:
- You've gained new skills
- Your priorities shift (family, location, etc.)
- You're considering a career change
- You've had experiences that changed your perspective

## Troubleshooting

**Q: Can I change an answer after submitting?**  
A: Yes! Use the "‚Üê Back" button to return and change previous answers.

**Q: What if I don't know how to answer?**  
A: Skip the question or choose the option closest to your feeling. You can retake anytime.

**Q: Can I see all questions before starting?**  
A: No, questions are shown one at a time for focused thinking. But you can navigate back and forth.

**Q: How are match scores calculated?**  
A: Scores combine multiple factors: your stated preferences, career stage, work style, values, and deal-breakers.

**Q: What if my top match doesn't feel right?**  
A: Use the full list of recommendations. The top match is algorithmic - you know yourself best! Also consider retaking with adjusted answers.

**Q: Can I export my results?**  
A: Yes! Results are automatically saved to `data/career_interview_results.json`. You can copy this file or use the "Save Results" button.
